The rapid expansion of AI data centers often occurs under the veil of Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs), preventing communities from knowing about these significant developments. This lack of transparency can lead to unforeseen consequences for local environments, economies, and the public's overall well-being. Residents deserve to be informed and engaged in decisions that impact their lives and surroundings.

 

A moratorium on the construction of new AI data centers is essential to establish a democratic process that ensures community input and oversight. We must prioritize informed discussions about the implications of these facilities on our neighborhoods, infrastructure, and resources. Without proper dialogue, we risk compromising the values and needs of our communities.

 

Now is the time to advocate for a pause on AI data center construction until a framework for transparency and community engagement is established. Together, we can create a responsible path forward that prioritizes public interests and fosters sustainable development in line with democratic principles.
