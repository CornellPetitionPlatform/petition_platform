The state needs to take action to allow the delivery of medical marijuana from local dispensaries. Patients suffering from cancer and other eligible conditions often face significant challenges in accessing their necessary medications. Delivery services would alleviate these barriers and ensure that individuals can receive their medicine in a timely and convenient manner.

 

Access to medical marijuana should not be a privilege limited by physical ability or transportation issues. By permitting dispensaries to offer delivery services, the state can enhance the quality of life for many patients who rely on these medications for relief from their symptoms.

 

This change would not only foster greater patient independence but also demonstrate a commitment to supporting those in need of medical care. It is essential to prioritize the health and well-being of all individuals reliant on medical marijuana for their treatment.
