Healthcare costs continue to burden countless families, making it critical to address the lack of price transparency in medical services. Implementing capped prices for lab tests and imaging not only promotes fairness but also ensures that patients aren’t caught off guard by exorbitant bills. It is time for hospitals to provide real upfront pricing, allowing patients to make informed decisions about their care.

 

Expanding financial assistance eligibility is essential to help those struggling to navigate rising costs. By ensuring that more people have access to financial aid, we can alleviate some of the pressures that prevent individuals from seeking necessary medical treatment. 

 

Furthermore, standardizing insurance coverage for preventive care will lead to healthier communities by encouraging early interventions. By prioritizing these changes, we can create a more equitable healthcare system that truly serves the needs of all individuals.
