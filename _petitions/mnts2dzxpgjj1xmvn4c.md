Child welfare is in crisis, and immediate reform is essential. Too many foster children are forced to sleep in offices or transport their belongings in trash bags, highlighting a system that fails to protect our most vulnerable. Every child deserves a safe and loving environment, not a makeshift solution that perpetuates their trauma.

It is crucial to establish more permanent placements for foster children, ensuring they have stable, nurturing homes where they can thrive. This includes increasing resources for foster families and providing comprehensive support to help children transition smoothly into their new lives.

By prioritizing the well-being of foster children and demanding systemic changes, we can create a future where every child has the opportunity to grow up in a safe, supportive environment. It's time to take a stand for those who cannot advocate for themselves.
