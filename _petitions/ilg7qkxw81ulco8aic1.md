Paid parking in our local park has created barriers for many community members wishing to enjoy this essential green space. Families, individuals, and visitors alike are finding it increasingly difficult to access the park due to the added financial burden of parking fees. This change has not only discouraged regular visitors but has also limited the park’s inclusivity, making it less accessible for everyone.

Free parking would enhance the park's use and encourage a greater sense of community engagement. It would provide everyone, regardless of their financial situation, the opportunity to enjoy recreational activities, spend quality time with loved ones, and experience the natural beauty the park has to offer. By removing parking fees, we can foster an environment where people feel welcomed and included.

Reinstating free parking is a necessary step toward ensuring that our park remains a vibrant and welcoming space for all. It will promote healthier lifestyles, community gatherings, and the enjoyment of nature, which are essential for both physical and mental well-being. Let's work together to make the park accessible for everyone once again.
