The growing reliance on artificial intelligence in customer care has significantly compromised the quality of service provided to consumers. While AI can handle basic inquiries, it lacks the human touch necessary for understanding complex issues and addressing customer emotions. This has led to frustration for many consumers who seek real solutions rather than automated responses.

Moreover, the introduction of AI has resulted in a shortage of human agents on the front lines. Companies opting for automation often reduce their workforce, leaving consumers with insufficient support during critical times. Human representatives possess the ability to improvise and think creatively to resolve problems, something AI simply cannot replicate.

To preserve the integrity of customer service, it is essential that companies prioritize hiring human customer care agents. By investing in people rather than solely in technology, businesses can ensure timely, empathetic, and effective support for their customers, ultimately fostering loyalty and satisfaction.
