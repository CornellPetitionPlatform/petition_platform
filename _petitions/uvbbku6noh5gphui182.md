Access to contraception is essential for young adults who often encounter barriers that hinder their ability to obtain the contraceptive methods they want and need. This situation can increase the risk of unintended pregnancies and sexually transmitted infections, which can have lasting implications on their health and education.

  
Many college campuses have successfully implemented contraception vending machines, providing students with easy access to essential products like condoms, oral contraceptives, and emergency contraception. These machines not only promote safe sex practices but also empower students to take control of their reproductive health conveniently and discreetly.

  
Expanding access to contraception through the installation of vending machines on our campus is a vital step in supporting the well-being and autonomy of all students. It is time to ensure that everyone has the resources they need for responsible sexual health. Your support can make this crucial service a reality.
