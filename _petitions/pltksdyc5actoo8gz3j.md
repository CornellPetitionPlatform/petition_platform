The rising cost of college tuition is a barrier for countless individuals striving to better their lives through education. As technology and innovation continue to advance, access to quality education should not be limited to the privileged few. Making college education free for all citizens would empower a new generation of thinkers, innovators, and leaders who can contribute to society and the economy without being burdened by student debt.

The wealth accumulated by tech oligarchs, largely due to the very innovations that shape our future, could be redirected towards funding public education. This shift would not only rectify economic disparities but also ensure a well-educated workforce that can adapt to rapidly changing job markets. By investing in education, these influential figures can help foster a more equitable society and secure their own interests in sustainable capitalism.

It is essential to challenge the current economic structure and demand that those who have benefited immensely from it give back to the community. The time has come to advocate for free college education funded by those who have accumulated vast wealth through the very systems that are purportedly crumbling. Investing in education is an investment in the future of capitalism itself, where opportunities are not restricted to a select few.
